The Billy Mays Tribute Salesmen Collective

...but WAIT! There's More!

Why Mays?

The Mays Constitution: We the followers of Mays, will always sell the best products with the greatest enthusiasm. All of our patrons' hair should be flown back by the power of our voices. We are no longer our former selves. We are a Billy Mays.

How We Mays?

Mays All Days!!: To be a solid BM, you must live the part at all times. Throw out all clothes that are not a blue button-down or a pair of khakis (it was well known that Billy Mays never wore underwear). Stop shaving all the damn time. We must become BM.

Become a Mays!

I'm Mays, Too!: Does the Mays lifestyle appeal to you? If so, you can get started immediately. Simply find a product - perhaps this fine flexbox - and a customer, and start selling your stuff in the BM uniform. Once you've begun, please send $1000/month to the BMTSC.
